Consider two sets \(A = \{{1, 9, 2, 5, 6, 7}\}\) and \(B = \{{5, 7, 4, 3, 1, 6}\}\). Calculate the symmetric difference between the sets \(A \cup B\) and \(A - B\). Draw a Venn diagram and mark the region \(X \Delta Y\).

    
    \hfill \textbf{(5)}
    \vspace{0.1in}


    \question
We define the universal set \( U = \{x | x \text{ is a natural number}\} \). Let \( A = \{{9, 8, 0, 2, 1, 7}\} \) and \( B = \{{0, 8, 7, 5, 4, 2}\} \). Then compute the set \( A^c - B^c \).

    
    \hfill \textbf{(5)}
    \vspace{0.1in}

    \question
    Consider two sets \(A = \{{4, 5, 9, 7, 8, 1}\}\) and \(B = \{{0, 8, 7, 6, 4, 3}\}\). 
    Write down two relations which are a function from X into Y such that
    \begin{itemize}
    \item Injective (One One) but not Surjective (Onto)
    \item Surjective (Onto) but not Injective (One One)
    \end{itemize}

    
    \hfill \textbf{(5)}
    \vspace{0.1in}


    \question
    Consider the following game of two players. Here row-player has action set \(\{A, B, C\}\) and column-player has action set \(\{D, E, F\}\).
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{c:c:c:c:}
 & D & E & F \\
\hdashline
A & (47, 48) & (46, 48) & (17, 3) \\
\hline
B & (35, 18) & (32, 9) & (10, 10) \\
\hline
C & (42, 43) & (16, 37) & (2, 13) \\
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Calculate the best responses for the row player when the column player chooses D.
    \item Calculate the best responses for the column player when the row player chooses A.
    \end{itemize}

    
    \hfill \textbf{(5)}
    \vspace{0.1in}


\end{questions}
